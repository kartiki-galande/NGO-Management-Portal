Education

Happy to Help Society,Pune.


The Aadhar Kendra or Sponsorship programme aims at shaping a positive future for children from the marginalised community who cannot afford the basic necessities of life. While Happy to Help reaches out to many children within the community; we have identified a particularly vulnerable group: those children who have lost one or both parents.
Although many orphaned children are taken in by extended families, the extra burden on family resources means that they are often required to work, cutting short their childhood.
The Aadhar Kendra programme wants to provide their children with the best possible start to their life.
In an increasingly globalised world, quality education has become vital for children to be able to progress.
