About Us

Happy to Help Society..

Happy to Help is an independent charitable organisation working to better the lives of low income, informal communities in Kolhapur, India.
DGS works with participants in and around Pune through a range of family welfare programmes encompassing education, environment, child development and women’s empowerment also for disaster relief.
Making the leadership platform for the students in rural area and devloping those rural areas using sustainable development goals is the main priority of HappytoHelp.
Our Vision: Empowerment of the marginalised through capacity building and sustainable rural and urban development and programmes.

The Community First..

About last 5 years, HappytoHelp has developed a strong rapport with the community by ensuring that community members have a platform to participate in the initiation and development of programmes. This ensures that programmes are relevant and valued by the community. HappytoHelp’s field workers are present in community every day to follow up with participants and ensure their opinions and experiences are fed back to the HappytoHelp’s decision makers.
One of our aims is to always ensure that our funds are spent where they are most needed. With this in mind we have been responsive to the changing needs of the community, and to the work other organisations so we can avoid duplicating services.
