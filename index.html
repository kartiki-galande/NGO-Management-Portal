Happy to Help is an independent charitable organisation working to better the lives of low income, informal communities in rural area of Kolhapur, India.

Our Focus Areas

Education
Education is seen as a highly effective empowering tool in our country. Educating children and young generation of India is very important task. We are taking various educational program for children.

Women Empowering
"A strong woman is one who feels deeply and loves fiercely. Her tears flow as abundantly as her laughter." Taking some steps towards women empowerment through various activities and inspiring others.

Disaster Relief
Each of us as human beings has a responsibility to reach out to help our brothers and sisters affected by disasters. We are trying to reach to the people who are facing these disastraous situations and helping them to overcome it.

Environment
Progress is impossible without change, and those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything. Trying to make the change in environment such that all animals and humans can live here without any hesitation or uncomfertability.

Our Featured Projects


20 December 2017
Children Education Program
"Children has the pure heart". On 20th December 2017, we arranged a children education program in a rural school at Tikkewadi, Kolhapur. We gathered the children from this rural area and convey them the importance of good education in very enthusiastically and creative ways by playing with them. The event was a good success.
01 March 2018
Youth Empowerment Program
According to population count, India has more population in youth. Using this youth power in right direction is very important. On 1st March, we arranged a Youth Empowerment program for the young people in the village. The professonal speaker guided them various paths for their career and importance of choosing the right path in life.
05 August 2019
Helping in Flood Areas
August 2019 Flood was very dangerous for Kolhapur and Satara districts in Maharashtra. Giving first help to the people who were in that situation is very important. Our volunteers were helping the people in need in those areas. Volunteers helped people in rescue mission as well as for their food, their shelter like basic needs. This help was very important at that time.
